Biography

Monkey D Luffy is the Anime/Manga character created by Oda Sensei. He's one of the most lovable character and undoubtedly most epic character.Born in Foosha Village, Luffy is the son of Monkey D. Dragon, the leader of the Revolutionary Army,[32] and the grandson of the Marine hero Monkey D. Garp,[33] and their family carries the initial and Will of D. At age 7, Luffy accidentally ate the Gomu Gomu no Mi, which turned his body into rubber.
